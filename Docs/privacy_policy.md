### 2. Toplanan Veriler

#### a) Reklam Hizmetleri (Google AdMob)
Uygulamamız, ücretsiz olarak sunulabilmek için Google AdMob reklam ağını kullanmaktadır. AdMob aşağıdaki verileri toplayabilir:

- **Reklam kimliği (Advertising ID):** Kişiselleştirilmiş reklamlar sunmak amacıyla kullanılır.
- **IP adresi:** Yaklaşık konum tespiti ve dolandırıcılık önleme amacıyla kullanılır.
- **Cihaz bilgileri:** İşletim sistemi sürümü, cihaz modeli gibi teknik bilgiler.
- **Uygulama kullanım verileri:** Hangi reklamların görüntülendiği ve tıklandığı.

Google AdMob'un gizlilik politikasına şuradan ulaşabilirsiniz:  
👉 https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### b) Uygulama İçi Veriler
Uygulamanın çalışması için aşağıdaki veriler **yalnızca cihazınızda** (yerel depolama) saklanır ve dışarıya aktarılmaz:

- Tamamlanan seviye bilgileri ve yıldız puanları
- Oyuncu coin (jeton) bakiyesi
- Satın alınan ve donatılan görsel öğeler (kostümler)
- Dil tercihleriniz

**Bu veriler hiçbir sunucuya gönderilmez.**

## 🇬🇧 English

### Crystal Mirror Privacy Policy

**App:** Crystal Mirror  
**Publisher:** EVN Labs (`com.evnlabs.crystalmirror`)  
**Contact:** evnlabs@gmail.com

---

### 1. Overview

Crystal Mirror is a mobile puzzle game that prioritizes user experience. This privacy policy explains what data the application collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

---

### 2. Data We Collect

#### a) Advertising Services (Google AdMob)
Our application uses the Google AdMob advertising network to remain free of charge. AdMob may collect the following data:

- **Advertising ID:** Used to serve personalized ads.
- **IP address:** Used for approximate location detection and fraud prevention.
- **Device information:** Technical details such as OS version and device model.
- **App usage data:** Which ads were displayed and clicked.

You can access Google AdMob's privacy policy here:  
👉 https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### b) In-App Data
The following data is stored **only on your device** (local storage) for the app to function and is never transmitted externally:

- Completed level information and star ratings
- Player coin balance
- Purchased and equipped cosmetic items (skins)
- Your language preferences

**This data is never sent to any server.**

---

### 3. User Account

You do **not** need to create any account to use Crystal Mirror. The application does **not** collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.).

---

### 4. Third-Party Services

The application uses only the following third-party service:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | Ad display | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

---

### 5. Children's Privacy

We did not design this application specifically for children under the age of 13. However, our AdMob configuration operates in child-directed mode to avoid serving ads targeted at users under 13.

---

### 6. Data Security

Local data is kept in encrypted storage via Android's Capacitor Preferences API. The app uses the internet connection only for loading advertisements.

---

### 7. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. For significant changes, a notification will be provided within the application. The current policy is always available in this document.

---

For questions regarding this privacy policy:
